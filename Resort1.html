A beautiful melange of nature and plush amenities, the Prakruthi Club, among the finest resorts around Bangalore beckons for a tranquil and relaxing experience. Located close to the Devanahalli airport,it stretches across 7 manicured and green acres. The refreshing environment and array of recreational avenues makes it an ideal getaway. Activity

BMX (bicycle motor cross) is a sport involving racing of purpose built bicycles across a dirt track with ramps and humps. We do not recommend it to the faint hearted. Bring out the X factor in this Extreme sport.

Zorbing (globe-riding, sphereing, orbing) is the recreation of rolling downhill in an orb, generally made of transparent plastic. At Prakruthi, we have harnessed zorbs that can spin two persons at a time. Please come and experience your world upside down.
